About & FAQ

Everything you need to know about YzemanBot

YzemanBot is a Telegram Mini App that rewards you with COINS for watching ads, referring friends, completing daily tasks, and competing in tournaments. The more active you are, the more you earn!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my referral link?
Go to the "Refer" tab in the app. Your unique referral link will be displayed there. Share it with friends! When they join using your link, you both earn COINS. You'll earn tier-based COINS (5-25 per referral) plus 2% lifetime commission on everything they earn!
How often can I spin the wheel?
You can spin the wheel once every 3 days. The timer on the wheel page shows exactly when your next spin is available. Prizes range from 50 to 2,000 COINS, and your tier multiplier applies (up to 3x for Platinum users)! That means a Platinum user could win up to 6,000 COINS in one spin!
What happens if I miss a day of daily reward?
If you miss a day, your streak resets to 1. But don't worry - you can always start building your streak again! The longer your streak, the more COINS you earn per day (up to +0.1 COINS per day, plus +1 COINS every 7 days). A 30-day streak earns you 7.20 COINS per day!
How do I increase my tier?
Your tier increases automatically when you reach the required number of referrals:
• Brute: 150 referrals → 0.35 COINS/ad
• Silver: 350 referrals → 0.50 COINS/ad
• Gold: 700 referrals → 0.75 COINS/ad
• Platinum: 1,500 referrals → 1.00 COINS/ad
More referrals = Higher tier = More COINS per ad (up to 5x more!)
How do I withdraw my COINS?
Go to the "Withdraw" tab, save your USDT (TRC-20) wallet address, and click "Request Withdrawal" once you have at least 100,000 COINS. Withdrawals are processed manually and reviewed by admins. The value of your COINS is dynamic and based on overall platform activity!
What are bonus codes and how do I get them?
Bonus codes are special codes that give you extra COINS! Join our Telegram channel (@YzemanEarnBotChannel) to get notified about new bonus codes. Codes are also sometimes shared in the app's broadcast messages. Enter them in the "Bonus" tab to redeem instantly!
Is there a limit to how many ads I can watch?
NO! You can watch unlimited ads. The more you watch, the more COINS you earn! There's no daily limit, no hourly limit - watch as many as you want. Every ad gives you base COINS plus random bonus chances (2x, 5x, 10x, or even 25x!).
How does the 2% referral commission work?
When someone you referred earns COINS (from ads, wheel spins, daily rewards, etc.), you automatically get 2% of their earnings. This is LIFETIME commission - you keep earning as long as they keep earning. The more successful your referrals are, the more you earn passively!
What is the value of 100,000 COINS?
Currently, 100,000 COINS is valued at approximately $2,000 USD. However, the value is DYNAMIC and can INCREASE as more users join and the platform grows! The value is determined by total ad revenue divided by total COINS in circulation. Early adopters benefit the most!
How do I join a team?
Go to the "Team" tab in the app. You can either:
• Create your own team (costs nothing!)
• Join an existing team using a 6-character team code
Teams compete monthly for prizes - top 3 teams give EVERY member 2,500/1,000/500 COINS!
How do I win tournament prizes?
First, join the tournament in the "Tournament" tab. Then simply earn COINS as you normally would - every COINS you earn during the week counts toward your tournament score. Top 3 at the end of the week (Sunday) win 2,000/1,000/500 COINS. Prizes are awarded automatically on Monday!
What are the random ad bonuses?
Every ad you watch has a chance to give you a random multiplier:
• 15% chance for 2x (Lucky Ad) ✨
• 5% chance for 5x (Golden Ad) ⭐
• 1% chance for 10x (MEGA Ad) 🌟
• 0.2% chance for 25x (EPIC Ad!) 🔥
This means you could earn 25x your normal reward from a single ad! Keep watching - you never know when you'll get lucky!
How is the 100,000 COINS value calculated?
The value is calculated using this formula:
Value = (Total Ad Revenue × 0.7) ÷ Total COINS in Circulation
• 70% of ad revenue goes to the reward pool
• 30% goes to platform operations and growth
As more users join and watch ads, both revenue and COINS increase, but the value can go UP or DOWN based on activity!
When do monthly leaderboards reset?
Monthly leaderboards reset on the 1st day of every month at 00:00 UTC. Everyone starts at zero, giving you a fresh chance to win every month! Top 3 earners each month win 5,000 / 3,000 / 1,000 COINS.
Is my data safe?
YES! Your data is securely stored and never shared with third parties. We only use your Telegram ID, name, and username to identify you in the app. Your wallet address is stored securely and only used for withdrawals.
