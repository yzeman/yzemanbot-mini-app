Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my referral link?
Go to the "Refer" tab in the app. Your unique referral link will be displayed there. Share it with friends! When they join using your link, you both earn COINS.
How often can I spin the wheel?
You can spin the wheel once every 3 days. The timer shows exactly when your next spin is available.
What happens if I miss a day of daily reward?
Your streak resets to 1 day. But you can always start building your streak again! The longer your streak, the more COINS you earn per day.
How do I increase my tier?
Your tier increases automatically when you reach the required number of referrals. More referrals = Higher tier = More COINS per ad!
How do I withdraw my COINS?
Go to the "Withdraw" tab, save your USDT (TRC-20) wallet address, and click "Request Withdrawal" once you have at least 100,000 COINS.
What are bonus codes and how do I get them?
Bonus codes are special codes that give you extra COINS. Join our Telegram channel (@YzemanEarnBotChannel) to get notified about new bonus codes!
Is there a limit to how many ads I can watch?
No! You can watch unlimited ads. The more you watch, the more COINS you earn!
How does the 2% referral commission work?
When someone you referred earns COINS, you get 2% of their earnings automatically. This is lifetime commission - you earn as long as they earn!
