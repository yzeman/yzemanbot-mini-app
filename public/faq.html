Inactive Account Policy

To keep the platform fair and save database space, inactive accounts are managed as follows:

  • ⏰ 4 Months Inactive - COINS reset to 0 and history cleared. Your account, referrals, and tier remain intact. You'll get a welcome back message when you return!
  • 🗑️ 12 Months Inactive - Account and all associated data permanently deleted.
  • ✅ Stay Active - Just open the app at least once every 4 months to keep your coins safe!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my referral link?
Go to the "Refer" tab. Your unique referral link is displayed there. Share it! When friends join, you earn 50-500 COINS each + 2% lifetime commission on their earnings.
How much can I earn per ad?
Fresher: 5, Brute: 10, Silver: 15, Gold: 25, Platinum: 50 COINS per ad. Plus lucky bonuses: 2x (15%), 5x (5%), 10x (1%), 25x (0.2%)! A Platinum user hitting EPIC earns 1,250 COINS from ONE ad!
How often can I spin the wheel?
Once every 3 days. Prizes range from 200 to 5,000 COINS. Your tier multiplier applies (up to 3x for Platinum)!
What happens if I miss a daily reward?
Your streak resets to 1. But you can always rebuild! Streak bonuses: +0.5/day, +10 every 7 days, 30 days = +1,000, 100 days = +5,000.
How do I withdraw my COINS?
Go to "Withdraw" tab, save your USDT (TRC-20) wallet, and request withdrawal at 1,000,000 COINS. Current value: ~$2,000 USD — this can INCREASE as more users join!
How do I join the tournament?
Go to the "Tournament" tab and click "Join Tournament". All COINS you earn during the week count. Top 3 win 8,000/5,000/3,000 COINS. Prizes awarded Monday 00:00 UTC.
How does tournament chat work?
Once you join the tournament, a chat button appears. Tap it to chat with other participants. You can edit or delete your own messages. Messages auto-delete after 24 hours.
How does the 2% referral commission work?
When someone you referred earns COINS (ads, wheel, daily, achievements, prizes), you get 2% automatically. This is LIFETIME — you earn as long as they earn!
Can I chat with other users?
Yes! Private chat with friends, team chat with teammates, and tournament chat with participants. All have real-time messaging, edit/delete, and profanity filters.
What happens if I'm inactive?
After 4 months inactive: coins reset to 0, history cleared, but account/referrals/tier kept. You get a welcome back message when you return. After 12 months: account fully deleted. Stay active to keep your coins!
Is there a limit to how many ads I can watch?
No! Unlimited ads. More ads = more COINS + more chances for lucky bonuses. Hit milestones at 5K (+30K COINS), 10K (+100K COINS) for massive rewards!
Is my privacy protected?
Absolutely! Your username is never shown publicly — only your first name. Other users cannot see your Telegram ID. You control who you accept as friends.
