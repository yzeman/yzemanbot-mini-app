Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my referral link?
Go to the "Refer" tab in the app. Your unique referral link will be displayed there. Share it with friends! When they join using your link, you both earn COINS. You'll earn tier-based COINS (5-25 per referral) plus 2% lifetime commission on everything they earn!
How do I send a friend request?
Go to your Profile page, then tap the "Find" tab. Search for users by name, then click the "+" button next to their name to send a friend request. Once they accept, you can chat privately!
How does private chat work?
Once you're friends with someone, you can tap the message icon next to their name in your friends list. This opens a private chat where you can send real-time messages. You'll see a notification badge on the message icon when someone sends you a new message!
Can I block someone?
Yes! You can block any user from their profile page or from your friends/requests list. Blocked users cannot send you friend requests or messages. Blocking also affects their trust score.
What is the Trust Score?
Your Trust Score is calculated based on referrals, earnings, and blocks received. Higher scores mean you're a trusted community member. When someone receives a friend request from a user with a low trust score (below 30), they'll see a warning before accepting.
How often can I spin the wheel?
You can spin the wheel once every 3 days. The timer on the wheel page shows exactly when your next spin is available. Prizes range from 50 to 2,000 COINS, and your tier multiplier applies (up to 3x for Platinum users)!
What happens if I miss a day of daily reward?
If you miss a day, your streak resets to 1. But don't worry - you can always start building your streak again! The longer your streak, the more COINS you earn per day (up to +0.1 COINS per day, plus +1 COINS every 7 days).
How do I increase my tier?
Your tier increases automatically when you reach the required number of referrals. More referrals = Higher tier = More COINS per ad! For example, a Platinum user (1,500+ referrals) earns 1.0 COINS per ad, while a Fresher earns only 0.2 COINS per ad. That's 5x more earnings!
How do I withdraw my COINS?
Go to the "Withdraw" tab, save your USDT (TRC-20) wallet address, and click "Request Withdrawal" once you have at least 100,000 COINS. The value of your COINS is dynamic and based on overall platform activity!
What are bonus codes and how do I get them?
Bonus codes are special codes that give you extra COINS! Join our Telegram channel (@YzemanEarnBotChannel) to get notified about new bonus codes. Enter them in the "Bonus" tab to redeem instantly!
Is there a limit to how many ads I can watch?
No! You can watch unlimited ads. The more you watch, the more COINS you earn! Every ad gives you base COINS plus random bonus chances (2x, 5x, 10x, or even 25x!).
How does the 2% referral commission work?
When someone you referred earns COINS (from ads, wheel spins, daily rewards, etc.), you automatically get 2% of their earnings. This is LIFETIME commission - you keep earning as long as they keep earning. The more successful your referrals are, the more you earn passively!
How do I join a team?
Go to the "Team" tab in the app. You can either create your own team (costs nothing!) or join an existing team using a 6-character team code. Once in a team, you can chat with teammates and compete in monthly team competitions!
How do I win tournament prizes?
First, join the tournament in the "Tournament" tab. Then simply earn COINS as you normally would - every COINS you earn during the week counts toward your tournament score. Top 3 at the end of the week (Sunday) win 2,000/1,000/500 COINS. Prizes are awarded automatically on Monday!
What are the random ad bonuses?
Every ad you watch has a chance to give you a random multiplier: 15% chance for 2x (Lucky Ad), 5% chance for 5x (Golden Ad), 1% chance for 10x (MEGA Ad), 0.2% chance for 25x (EPIC Ad!). This means you could earn 25x your normal reward from a single ad!
Is my privacy protected?
Absolutely! We take privacy seriously. Your username is never displayed publicly - only your first name is shown. Other users cannot see your Telegram ID or contact you outside the app. You have full control over who you accept as friends.
