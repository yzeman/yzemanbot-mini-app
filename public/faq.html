Getting Started
Welcome to YzemanBot! Your journey to earning COINS starts here.
🤔 What is YzemanBot?

YzemanBot is a Telegram-based mini-app that allows you to earn COINS by watching ads, referring friends, completing daily tasks, spinning the wheel, and participating in competitions.

COINS can be withdrawn to your USDT (TRC-20) wallet once you reach the minimum withdrawal amount.

🎮 How do I start earning?

Getting started is easy! Just follow these steps:

  1. Open the YzemanBot mini-app from Telegram
  2. Your account is automatically created
  3. Go to the Earn tab and click "Watch Ad"
  4. Watch a short video to earn your first COINS
  5. Invite friends to earn bonus COINS
  6. Check in daily for streak bonuses
